The Importance of Cybersecurity

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These attacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information, extorting money, or interrupting normal business processes.

Why is it Important?

With the rise of technology, businesses and individuals store a lot of important information online. Cybersecurity helps protect this data from unauthorized access or harm. Without strong cybersecurity measures, personal information, business secrets, and even health records could be at risk.

How Can You Stay Safe?

  • Use strong passwords.
  • Keep your software updated.
  • Be careful with suspicious emails or links.
  • Use antivirus software and firewalls.
